### I'm Jonathan Wise

I've been involved with manufacturing information systems, analytics and bridging the OT/IT divide for more than 25 years. Today we call this "smart manufacturing" or "Industry 4.0" and I work with some of the organizations leading the charge, including [CESMII - The Smart Manufacturing Institute](https://www.cesmii.org), the [OPC Foundation](https://www.opcfoundation.org) and [Manufacturing-X](https://factory-x.org/manufacturing-x/). 

I'm the Chief Technology Architect at CESMII, which is the United States' national institute focused on this space, and a department of [UCLA's Office of Advanced Research Computing](https://oarc.ucla.edu/about/mission), where I've contributed to both [curriculum](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0128200278/) and [patent creation](https://patents.google.com/patent/US12299623B2/). 

I'm also a [smart manufacturing consultant](https://www.acetechnologies.net/) for manufacturers working on their own digital transformations.

Among other things, I advocate for open and common technologies, standards, and best practices that can help everyone do manufacturing smarter and more efficiently.
